Key Technologies

Sensors: Allow robots to perceive their environment.
Actuators: Enable robots to move and manipulate objects.
Computer Vision: Allows robots to process and understand visual information.
AI and Machine Learning: Enables robots to learn from experience and make decisions.

Challenges and Future Directions

Improving robot dexterity and fine motor skills
Enhancing human-robot interaction and collaboration
Developing more advanced and efficient power sources
Addressing ethical concerns about robot autonomy and decision-making
